🚀 PrestaShop Connector for Odoo 19

Stable Sync. Predictable Flows. ERP-Centric Design.

This app connects PrestaShop and Odoo 19 using PrestaShop's official integration interface, enabling a reliable and structured connection between your online store and ERP.

Built for businesses that want PrestaShop to work cleanly with Odoo, without brittle customizations or tightly coupled workflows.

🎯 What Makes This App Different?

Most PrestaShop connectors try to cover every possible scenario. This connector focuses on getting the core ERP flows right.

  • Odoo remains the system of record for operations
  • No custom checkout hacks or theme dependencies
  • No webhook-based black-box behavior
  • Designed for long-term maintainability

Just a transparent, predictable, and upgrade-safe integration between PrestaShop and Odoo.

🔁 Core Functional Capabilities

📦 Product Synchronization

  • Import products from PrestaShop into Odoo
  • Support for combinations (variants)
  • SKU mapping and variant identification
  • Clean product structure aligned with Odoo Product models

🛒 Order Management

  • Import PrestaShop orders as Odoo Sales Orders
  • Support for discounts, shipping charges, and taxes
  • Clear linkage between PrestaShop and Odoo documents
  • Sales orders ready for Odoo invoicing workflows

🚚 Inventory Synchronization

  • Stock updates from Odoo to PrestaShop
  • Scheduled synchronization via Odoo cron jobs
  • Manual sync available for controlled operations (debug mode)
  • Prevents stock mismatches between ERP and storefront

🏢 Multi-store Support

  • Support for PrestaShop multistore environments
  • Mapping of PrestaShop stores with Odoo instances
  • Store-specific order and product synchronization

💰 Accounting-Friendly Design

  • Sales orders structured for clean downstream accounting
  • Compatible with standard Odoo Invoicing flows
  • No forced accounting customizations

⚙ Full Sync Control & Transparency

  • Scheduled synchronization for automation
  • Manual synchronization for testing and debugging
  • Detailed logs for every sync operation
  • Clear visibility into integration behavior

You always know what synced, when, and why.

🏗 Designed for Real-World Odoo Usage

  • Businesses running PrestaShop as their storefront
  • Teams using Odoo as their core ERP
  • Multi-store PrestaShop setups
  • Partners who value maintainable integrations
