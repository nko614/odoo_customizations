## How to use

1. **Open any Sales Order**
2. **In the order lines section**, click **"Create Custom Product"** 
3. **Fill in product details** (name, code, prices)
4. **Click "Next: Create BOM"**
5. **Add components** to the bill of materials
6. **Click "Create BOM & Add to Order"**
7. **Done!** The custom product is now on your sale order line
